About Me

Little me had dreams of growing into some sort of rock star astronaut. I was the kind of child who won science fairs, creative writing awards, and music competitions. I had wide-ranging interests which didn't quite coalesce into one clear path to the future. As a young adult in the 1990s, I ran after a passion for music and creative self-expression. All other interests and aptitudes were set aside. I found my way through loft parties, dive bars, jazz clubs and beautiful old auditoriums. I've been looking for a bit of the magic I've always felt listening to music written by others. I'm still on that journey.

Years went by. I worked day jobs to pay the bills, while rehearsing and performing up to six nights a week. All other interests and ambitions waited in the wings, like unanswered questions. Here and there, I saw signs that I could do more to realize my potential and help others. I began to realize that my life is about more than doing one thing well. It is now clear that my nature is to explore a variety of topics, and to always be learning. Improving my ability in any one area helps me develop in all areas. This best positions me to make a positive impact on my community in the long run.

In 2018 I began my intensive study of user experience design and front-end development. My efforts are ongoing, and when I do finally succeed I will know exactly why. I can bring all of this and more to your projects.
